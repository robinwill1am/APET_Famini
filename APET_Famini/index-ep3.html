Examples of Emerging Trends

Customer Data Platforms

Customer data platforms bring this information together into a single source to provide a comprehensive picture of consumers and eliminate the potential for unclean data.

Think of customer data as precious nuggets of gold. Now, imagine these nuggets scattered in different places, hard to track and use effectively. That's where customer data platforms (CDPs) come in. They're like the vault where you store and organize this valuable gold, so you can create personalized experiences for customers.









Cybersecurity Mesh

Cybersecurity mesh is a completely different approach to digital security. Cybersecurity mesh allows companies to create security measures based on the identity of a device, rather than proximity to secure connections.

In the world of digital security, things have changed. Traditional methods weren't ready for the remote work explosion during the pandemic. Enter cybersecurity mesh – it's like building a security web around every device, no matter where it is. This web is super smart, identifying devices by their "digital fingerprints" rather than where they're physically located.

Digital Health

If COVID-19 left a legacy that will remain, it’s digital health. As patients became unable to visit doctors’ offices and hospitals, digital health rose to fill the gap. But now that hurting or sick individuals have realized there are opportunities for receiving appropriate healthcare from their providers without in-person visitation, they are taking advantage of that opportunity.

Picture a world where you can receive top-notch medical care without leaving your home. Thanks to digital health, that's a reality. It's like bringing the doctor's office to you through technology. And guess what? It's here to stay, changing the way we take care of ourselves.

Imagine being part of this health-tech revolution, helping people access medical expertise from wherever they are, and even pioneering smart medical devices that keep us connected to our health.
